Adverb

An adverb is a word that modifies (describes) a verb (he sings loudly), an adjective (very tall), another adverb (ended too quickly), or even a whole sentence (Fortunately, I had brought an umbrella). An another base of type of Adverbs:-

1.Adverbs of Manner:-

An adverb of manner describes how you do an action. For example, They dress elegantly. Some elderly people drive slowly.

2.Adverbs of Place:-

Adverbs that are employed in a sentence to describe the location or the place where an action is taking place are called adverbs of place. They answer the question 'where'. They are mostly found after the main verb or the object in a sentence.
