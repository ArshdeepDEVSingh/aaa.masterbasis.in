Verb

Types of Verbs

There many types based on different situations but, most important types for student of class 8th are "regular" and "irregular".

Regular Verbs:-
FOR EXAMPLE:-
  • Happen - Happened - Happened
  • Talk - Talked - Talked
  • Help - Helped - Helped

These are the words in which "ed" is added at the last of the spelling when turning it to past tense.

Irregular Verbs-

These are the words which are totally changed when turning it to past tense.

FOR EXAMPLE:-
  • GO - Went - Gone
  • Get- Got - Gotten
  • See- Saw - Seen
