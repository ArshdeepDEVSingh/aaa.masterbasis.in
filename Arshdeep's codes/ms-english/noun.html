NOUN

Types of Nouns

  • Proper noun
  • Common Nouns
  • Material Nouns
  • Collective Nouns
  • Abstract Nouns

Proper Nouns:-

These are names of any pariticular person, thing, place, etc.

FOR EXAMPLE:- Arshdeep Singh, my Pen, India, (etc.).

{Also you can notice that first letter of Proper Nouns are capitel is on of the rule of ENGLISH GRAMMER}

Common Nouns:-

These are names which can be used for any common person, thing, place, etc.

FOR EXAMPLE:- boy, book, city, (etc.)

Material Nouns:-

These are names of things that are mattelic or non-mattelic.

FOR EXAMPLE:- iron, copper, wood, rubber, (etc.)

Collective Nouns-

These are names of collections of persons, things, places, etc.

FOR EXAMPLE:- Group of Fishes = School ; Group of Soldiers = Soldiers

Group of States = Country ; Group of Flowers = Bouquet

Astract Nouns:-

These are names of things that cannot be seen, but only felt.

FOR EXAMPLE:- air, sound, light, happiness, sadness, (etc.).
